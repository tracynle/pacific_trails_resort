What is a Yurt?

What is a yurt?

Yurts originated in Mongolia and have been widely used for thousands of years. It is a portable tent made out of a wooden frame. It is typically covered with animal skins, felt, wood, or straw but its construction has changed over time in order to be more stable and long lasting. Now, yurts are designed to fit modern living.

Our luxury yurts are permanent structures four feet off the ground. Each yurt is fully enclosed with canvas walls, a wooden floor, and a roof dome that can be opened. It is an amazing structure classified as an unique experience to stay in for your vacation!

How are the yurts furnished?

Each yurt is furnished with a queen-sized bed with down quilt and gas-fire stove. Your luxury camping experience includes electricity and a sink with hot and cold running water.

Enjoy our modern yurt tents in the great outdoors!

What Should I Bring?

Please check the weather conditions to pack accordingly, but we do recommend bringing few items that would give you a sense of preparation and comfort during your stay:

  1. Light and breathable layers of clothing
  2. Comfortable hiking boots or trail runners
  3. Sunglasses
  4. A lightweight hat
  5. A flashlight
  6. A sense of adventure!
