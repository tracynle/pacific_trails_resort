Activities

Activities at Pacific Trails Resort

Hiking

Pacific Trails Resort has 5 miles of hiking trails and is adjacent to a state park. Go it alone or join one of our guided hikes.

Kayaking

Ocean kayaks are available for guest use. Visit the guest services to reserve your kayak to rent by the hour.

Bird Watching

While anytime is a good time for bird watching at Pacific Trails, we offer guided bird-watching trips at sunrise several times a week.

Guided Tours

Enjoy our many guided tours that we offer that allows you to experience the coast. We have many that you can choose from. Sign up at the front-desk and we'd be happy to inform you the different tours available.
