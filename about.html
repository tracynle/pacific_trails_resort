Founders

In 2010, Pacific Trails Resorts was established when the Smiths' recalled their first experience in a yurt. After traveling with their family to Mongolia they wanted to bring modernity to this amazing structure to California so they could have it available for everyone to experience. Now thousands of travelers visit Pacific Trails Resort every year no matter what season!
